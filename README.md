# # 📞 Call Center Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🏢 Project Overview

This project presents a **Call Center Performance Dashboard** developed in Power BI to analyze operational efficiency, agent performance, and customer satisfaction.

The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders monitor key metrics in real-time and make **data-driven decisions** to improve service quality and optimize workforce management.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Call centers generate a large volume of data daily, but organizations often struggle to:

* Monitor real-time performance efficiently
* Identify underperforming agents
* Understand customer satisfaction trends
* Optimize staffing during peak hours
* Reduce call abandonment rates

Without a centralized dashboard, decision-making becomes slow and less effective.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The goal of this dashboard is to:

* Provide a **centralized view** of call center operations
* Track key performance indicators (KPIs)
* Analyze **agent-level performance**
* Understand **customer satisfaction patterns**
* Identify **peak call hours and busy days**
* Support **operational and strategic decisions**

---

## 🔍 Key Business Questions

This dashboard answers the following:

1. What is the total number of calls received?
2. How efficient is the call resolution process?
3. What is the current customer satisfaction level (CSAT)?
4. Which agents are top performers and which need improvement?
5. When are the peak hours for incoming calls?
6. Which days experience the highest call volumes?
7. How are calls distributed across satisfaction levels?

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1. 📞 Total Call Volume

* Displays the total number of calls received
* Provides a quick overview of workload

---

### 2. 🎯 KPI Metrics

Key performance indicators included:

* **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)**
* **Call Resolution Rate**
* **Call Abandonment Rate**
* **Average Speed of Answer**
* **Average Call Handling Time**

👉 These KPIs measure both **efficiency and service quality**

---

### 3. 👨‍💼 Agent Performance Analysis

* Compares performance across agents
* Metrics include:

  * Total calls handled
  * Resolution rate
  * CSAT score
  * Speed of response

👉 Helps identify:

* High-performing agents
* Agents requiring training

---

### 4. 😊 Customer Satisfaction Analysis

* Breaks down calls into:

  * Very Satisfied
  * Satisfied
  * Neutral
  * Dissatisfied
  * Very Dissatisfied
  * Not Served

👉 Provides insight into customer experience and service quality

---

### 5. ⏰ Call Volume by Hour

* Identifies peak call hours
* Highlights workload distribution throughout the day

👉 Useful for:

* Shift planning
* Workforce optimization

---

### 6. 📅 Call Volume by Day

* Shows busiest days of the week
* Helps allocate resources efficiently

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Call volume peaks during mid-day hours, indicating high demand periods
* High resolution rate reflects strong operational efficiency
* CSAT score suggests room for improvement in customer experience
* Call abandonment rate highlights potential service gaps
* Performance variation among agents indicates training opportunities
